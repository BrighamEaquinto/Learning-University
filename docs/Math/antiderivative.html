1.) \(F(x) = \int{\cos{(2x)}dx}\)

2.) Let \(u = 2x\)

3.) \(F(x) = \int{\cos{(u)}dx}\)

4.) Take the derivative of each side \(\frac{d}{dx}(u) = \frac{d}{dx}(2x)\)


5.) Now we have \(du = 2dx\)


6.) Solve for \(dx \rightarrow\) \(dx = \frac{du}{2}\)

7.) \(F(x) = \int{\cos{(u)} \times \frac{du}{2}}\)


8.) \(F(x) = \frac{\sin{(u)}}{2}\)
